Swivel Internal Websites & Marketing Pages

Designed and optimized responsive corporate websites and marketing landing pages for multiple Swivel Group brands, including Swivel Group, Swivel Tech, Swivel Finance, and Swivel Foundation. Working closely with the Lead UX Designer, marketing team, and company co-founders, I helped transform outdated marketing experiences into modern, conversion-focused platforms that improved user engagement and strengthened brand presence across digital channels.

The Challenge

The existing marketing websites were not generating the expected level of user engagement and lead conversions. Navigation structures, content presentation, and user journeys required significant improvement to better communicate the company's value proposition and encourage user action.

Additionally, the team wanted to introduce modern AI-powered capabilities while maintaining a consistent experience across multiple brand websites.

My Role & Responsibility

Working closely with the Lead UX Designer, I contributed across the full design process:

Collaborated with the Lead UX Designer throughout the end-to-end design process

Designed responsive web, tablet, and mobile experiences

Created multiple design explorations and compared alternative solutions to identify the most effective direction

Built interactive prototypes for stakeholder reviews and usability validation

Worked closely with developers and QA teams to ensure smooth implementation and design consistency

UX Process

01
Research
  • Gathered requirements from stakeholders, marketing teams, and company leadership.
  • Reviewed existing websites to identify usability issues, conversion barriers, and content gaps.
  • Analyzed competitor experiences and industry best practices.
02
Define
  • Created user personas and mapped primary user journeys.
  • Defined information architecture and content hierarchy.
  • Identified key conversion opportunities and business objectives.
03
Ideate
  • Collaborated with the Lead UX Designer and marketing team to explore multiple design directions.
  • Generated alternative concepts for critical landing page sections and conversion-focused components.
  • Evaluated solutions based on usability, business goals, and stakeholder feedback.
04
Design
  • Developed low-fidelity wireframes and high-fidelity UI designs.
  • Established consistent design patterns and reusable components.
  • Designed responsive experiences across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
05
Prototype
  • Created interactive prototypes to demonstrate user flows and validate design decisions.
  • Presented solutions to stakeholders and incorporated iterative feedback.
06
Testing & Handoff
  • Conducted internal reviews with the UX Lead, marketing team, and co-founders.
  • Refined designs through multiple feedback cycles.
  • Delivered detailed specifications and collaborated with developers and QA teams during implementation.

The Solution

  • Redesigned four corporate and marketing websites across the Swivel Group ecosystem.

  • Delivered fully responsive experiences for desktop, tablet, and mobile platforms.

  • Enhanced lead generation and contact workflows with improved forms and conversion-focused content.

  • Integrated AI-powered engagement features to streamline user interactions.

  • Established consistent visual patterns and user experiences across multiple brand websites.

Outcome & Impact

40%

Increased lead conversion rates compared to previous website versions.

↑

Improved user engagement and overall reach across all company websites.

30%

Improved workflow efficiency through AI-powered features introduced across platforms.

4×

Strengthened brand consistency and digital presence across multiple business units.

Learnings

  • Collaborating alongside a Lead Designer sharpened my ability to align on design direction quickly while still contributing distinct creative perspectives

  • Designing across multiple sub-brands within one system reinforced the importance of flexible component libraries that maintain consistency without limiting expression

  • Stakeholder prototype reviews taught me to present design rationale alongside visuals - decisions land better when the thinking behind them is made explicit

  • Working with QA teams highlighted how small implementation gaps can erode the designed experience, making design-dev alignment a continuous process rather than a one-time handoff
